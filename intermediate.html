INTERMEDIATE'SWORKOUT ROUTINE

After working hard in the gym for several months,it's time to step your training up a notch to keep your gains coming.


At this point, you should have good exercise technique and be able to handle more weight on the bar.


This 4-day-per-week intermediate program increases reps and sets to stimulate new muscle growth. When they become too easy, you can gradually add more weight or more reps/sets.



Remember that soreness is not always an indicator of muscle growth. Now that you have some training experience, you may not get sore after every workout.


Equipment Required: Fully-equipped Gym.

Rest periods: 90-180 seconds for main movements and 60-90 seconds for accessories.

Intensity: Select a weight that allows you to complete thr prescribed reps while leaving about two solid reps in the tank

Day 1:Full body

  • Legs:barbell back squats-5 sets of 7-8 reps.
  • Chest:flat barbell bench press-5 sets of 5-9reps.
  • Back:seated cable rows-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Soulders:seated dumbbell shoulder press-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Triceps:cable rope tricep pushdowns-4 sets of 8-10 reps.
  • Calves:seated calf raises-2 sets of 16-25 reps.
  • Abs:planks-3 sets of 40 seconds holds.


Day 2:Full body

  • Chest:dumbbell bench press-4 sets of 5-7 reps.
  • Back:dumbbell bent over rows-5 sets of 5 reps.
  • Chest:dumbbell fly-5 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Back:one-arm dumbbell rows-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Chest:pushups-3-4 sets of 8-10 reps.
  • Back/Chest:dumbbell pullovers-3 sets of 10-12 reps.


Day 3:Full body

  • Legs:barbell back squats-10 sets of 5 reps.
  • Chest:flat barbell bench press-7 sets of 5-7reps.
  • Back:seated cable rows-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Soulders:seated dumbbell shoulder press-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Triceps:cable rope tricep pushdowns-3 sets of 8-10 reps.
  • Calves:seated calf raises-3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Abs:planks-3 sets of 30 seconds holds.


Day 4:Upper/Lower body(as per requirement)

  • Legs:barbell back squats-9 sets of 5 reps.
  • Chest:flat barbell bench press-7 sets of 5-7reps.
  • Back:seated cable rows-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Soulders:seated dumbbell shoulder press-4 sets of 6-8 reps.
  • Triceps:cable rope tricep pushdowns-3 sets of 8-10 reps.
  • Calves:seated calf raises-3 sets of 10-12 reps.
  • Abs:planks-3 sets of 30 seconds holds.
